ODE Solver: Euler & Improved Euler

This was made for MA331 class Differential Equations in Washburn University and is meant to work offline.

Euler's method uses the slope at the current point to step forward: yn+1 = yn + h · f(xn, yn). Improved Euler (Heun's method) uses the average of two slopes for better accuracy: k₁ = f(xn, yn), k₂ = f(xn + h, yn + h·k₁), and yn+1 = yn + (h/2)(k₁ + k₂).
